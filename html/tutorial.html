Tutorial

Step 1: Pin World Wide Wishlist to your Browser

In order to quickly save a wish from any website you need to pin this extension to your chrome, meaning it will be displayed at the top right. To do that click on the little puzzle piece in the top right corner of your browser. Then click the pin needle so it's colour changes.

Step 2: Click on the Icon

Once you are on the product page of an item you want to save, just click the extension icon. A popup will open up.

Step 3: Choose the Product Image

Here you can select which product image you want to save. You can add/edit information and details here.

Step 4: Save your Wish

After entering all details you want to save you can save your wish. By default it saves to the wishlist you set as your default wishlist. If you select another wishlist from the dropdown menu it will save to that list instead. You can also move the wish arround later.

Step 5: Where are my Wishes saved?

You can go to your wishlists either via opening the popup (the little gift icon in the top left) or by right-clicking the icon and selecting "Open Wishlists".
