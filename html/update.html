Update 1.1.0

New Features

  • Wishlist Totals: You can now see the total ammount you'd have to spend for all wishes on your wishlist. This meant I had to refactor the price field, your old prices have been appended to the wish notes, if you want to re-add them you unfortunately have to do so manually, sorry :(
  • Wish sorting: You can now sort your wishes alphabetically, by date or price. Each wishlist remembers your sorting method.
  • Collapsing Popup: When your cursor moves outside the popup, the latter will temporarily collapse. This lets you better see price or other information.
  • Bugs: there are probably plenty new bugs to discover. Thanks a lot for helping me find them!
